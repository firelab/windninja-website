Usage

WindNinja is widely used by operational fire managers in the US and abroad

  • Software: Wildland Fire Decision Support System, FlamMap, Interagency Fuel Decision Support System, Prometheus, Wildfire Analyst
  • Fire personnel: Incident Meteorologists, Fire Behavior Analysts, Long-term Analysts, on-the-ground firefighters
  • Non-fire: wind energy, training search and rescue dogs, canyon incision on Mars, wind erosion, paragliding
  • 20+ peer-reviewed journal articles
